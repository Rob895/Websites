Why?:

There has never been a better time to take up playing a woodwind instrument. Now, there is an abundance of reasonable quality and affordable saxophones and clarinets, and an internet search reveals a vast and diverse range of musical repertoire and playing opportunities. About Rob:

Over 25 years performing experience in:

* Big band/dance bands
* Solo instrumental
* Saxophone & clarinet quartets
* Jazz trio/quartet/quintet
* Dixie/trad bands
* Function/tribute bands
* Pit orchestra/bands for theatre/musicals
* Wind orchestras/concert bands
* Symphony orchestras
* Original work studio recording

In addition to on-to-one tuition, Rob has managed a saxophone quartet and a jazz quartet, led big bands, and is a regular tutor on saxophone play-days.

Rob has attended many instrumental courses.
